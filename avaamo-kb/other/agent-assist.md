## Source: https://docs.avaamo.com/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist

Customer service agents play a crucial role in ensuring business success by addressing customer needs. However, many call centers rely on outdated technologies that make it challenging for agents to provide efficient and high-quality service. Fragmented systems force agents to spend extra time on manual tasks, reducing their productivity and prolonging customer wait times.

Avaamo Agent Assist offers a solution by leveraging Generative AI tools to streamline these processes. The system automates routine tasks, such as gathering information or suggesting responses, allowing agents to focus more on customer interaction. This reduces wait times, enhances service quality, and improves overall efficiency, leading to better customer experiences.

## 

Overview

Avaamo Agent Assist, powered by [LLaMB](/user-guide/llamb/overview-key-features), is a tool designed to improve agent performance by providing AI-driven assistance throughout customer interactions. From call initiation to resolution, the system integrates seamlessly with CCaaS (Contact Center as a Service) and CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platforms, delivering real-time AI-driven information and guidance to the agents. This saves time for both agents and customers, helping agents work more efficiently while providing accurate information that helps address customer needs faster.

## 

Key features

### 

Real-Time Guidance with Automatic Recommendations

The `Automatic Recommendations` feature in Avaamo Agent Assist provides agents with real-time, AI-driven guidance throughout the conversation, helping them respond quickly and effectively to customer needs. This system ensures that agents have relevant, contextual information at their fingertips, improving the quality and speed of customer interactions.

**Key benefits:**

1.  **Real-Time Responses**: As the customer speaks, the system displays suggested responses or scripts to guide the agent in real-time. This helps the agent provide accurate answers quickly, ensuring the conversation flows smoothly and reducing response times.
    
2.  **Contextual Suggestions**: The system tailors its recommendations based on specific information about the customer. For example, it considers factors such as the customer's geographic location, previous interactions, or the department they are interacting with, allowing the agent to deliver personalized, relevant responses.
    

With this feature, agents are supported in real time, ensuring that they provide timely and context-appropriate answers, leading to more efficient and effective customer service.

Refer [Automatic Recommendations,](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/recommendations) for more information.

### 

Enhancing Agent Efficiency with Knowledge Assist

The `Knowledge Assist` tool provides the flexibility to look up the knowledge manually at any time during or after a call. It allows users to access the entire knowledge repository from a single screen within the widget, thus providing a seamless knowledge search experience and enabling quick and efficient retrieval of relevant information beyond the scope of automated recommendations.

**Key benefits:**

1.  **Real-Time Knowledge Assist:** Agents can manually input queries at any point during or after a call, enabling quick and efficient retrieval of relevant information. This enhances their ability to focus on delivering accurate, context-aware solutions in real-time.
    
2.  **Agent Queries**: Agents can also ask direct questions during a call. The system quickly responds by searching integrated knowledge bases or CMSs for relevant answers, enabling fast access to necessary information.
    
3.  **Knowledge Sharing:** Once information is retrieved, agents can share the solutions in knowledge articles with customers. This ensures that the customer receives accurate and consistent information without delay.
    
4.  **Multi-Source Integrations**: AI Knowledge Assist can integrate with multiple content sources, including CMSs, and custom knowledge bases. It provides recommendations based on content specific to the company or customer, ensuring that the information shared is accurate and relevant.
    

This feature enables real-time access to your knowledge repository, allowing agents to retrieve and share accurate information during calls.

Refer [AI Knowledge Assist](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/knowledge-assist), for more information.

### 

Effortless Post-Call Management with Call Summarization

The `Call Summarization` feature in Avaamo Agent Assist simplifies agents' post-call efforts by creating concise summaries of lengthy conversations. It captures key points and decisions made during calls, ensuring essential information is easily accessible for future reference.

**Key benefits:**

1.  **Transcript Summarization**: The system automatically condenses lengthy conversations into short, digestible summaries. It focuses on key points and decisions, making it easier for agents to review the conversation and extract crucial information.
    
2.  **CRM Integrations**: Summaries can be automatically added to customer CRM records, ensuring a consistent and detailed history of interactions. This helps maintain comprehensive records, reducing the need for manual input from agents and improving overall data accuracy.
    

This feature saves time, improves information accuracy, and ensures that key insights are preserved for future use, enhancing customer service and record-keeping efficiency.

Refer [Call Summarization,](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/summary) for more information.

### 

Enhancing Conversations with Live Call Automated Transcription

The `Live Call Automated Transcription` feature in Avaamo Agent Assist offers real-time transcription of conversations during live calls. It instantly converts spoken words into text, allowing agents to follow the conversation more easily and eliminating the need to take notes. This live transcript helps agents keep track of key details, and it can be directly referenced during the call to ensure accuracy. Additionally, this transcript can be seamlessly integrated into CRM systems, maintaining an accurate and searchable record of customer interactions.

**Key benefits:**

1.  **Real-Time Transcription**: As the conversation unfolds, the system continuously generates a live text transcript for the agent's reference, providing clarity and making it easier to follow the conversation.
    
2.  **High Accuracy**: The system ensures highly accurate transcription, which is crucial for effectively operating other AI-driven features like automatic recommendations and call summaries. The high level of precision ensures that essential information is captured and relayed correctly, reducing errors and miscommunication.
    

This feature significantly enhances customer service interactions by improving agent efficiency and ensuring the accuracy of critical information. It enables agents to concentrate on addressing the caller's concerns rather than being preoccupied with capturing details, leading to more effective and focused problem-solving.

Refer [Live Call Automated Transcription](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/transcripts-view), for more information.

Below is the simple use case flow:

### 

**Pre-Call**

*   The agent logs in to the Agent Assist widget, ensuring they are ready to handle incoming calls. Refer [Login to Avaamo Agent Assist](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/get-started/login-to-avaamo-agent-assist), for more information.
    

### 

**On-Call**

*   The agent receives an incoming call from a user. Refer [Handling New Call Transcripts](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/transcripts-view#handling-new-call-transcripts), for more information.
    
*   As the user begins asking their query, the agent listens and responds accordingly.
    
*   The agent utilizes the [Knowledge Assist](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/knowledge-assist) and [Recommendations](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/recommendations) tabs to access relevant information and provide accurate responses.

*   A live conversation transcript is available, allowing the agent to reference key details during the call.
    

### 

**Post-Call**

*   After the call ends, the widget automatically redirects to the [Summary](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/summary) tab.
    
*   The agent reviews the summary of the last attended call, which includes key points and decisions.
    
*   The agent can transfer the summary directly to the integrated CRM or modify it before finalizing the entry.

## Source: https://docs.avaamo.com/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/recommendations

During an ongoing call, based on the caller's inputs, `Agent Assist` automatically searches the knowledge repository for relevant information and provides recommendations to help you respond to user queries. These recommendations include concise snippets along with citations, referencing content from the knowledge base. Typically, it takes about 4-6 seconds for recommendations to appear after the caller completes their input.

The `Recommendations View` displays a continuous feed of all recommendations generated throughout the conversation, with the most recent suggestions appearing at the top. Each recommendation also includes citations for the referenced knowledge resources, which you can click on to view the full knowledge article.

Agent Assist uses a knowledge base to quickly answer queries during calls This is populated using the **LLaMB** in the Avaamo platform, which ingests and structures relevant content for effective recommendations. Refer [Before you begin](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/get-started/before-you-begin), for more information.

**How Automatic Recommendations Work:**

1.  **Search for Relevant Answers**:
    
    As the agent engages in a call, Agent Assist reads the conversation in real-time, identifying user queries and searching for suitable responses within the knowledge library.
    
2.  **Real-Time Notifications for Recommendations**:
    
    *   If the agent is on a different tab or view when a recommendation is generated, a notification appears stating: `New recommendation received. Check the Recommendations tab.`
        
    *   This alert allows the agent to know that a recommended action or solution is available for the current user’s question.
        
    
3.  **Accessing Recommendations**:
    
    To view the recommendation, the agent navigates to the **Recommendations tab** where the proposed actions or solutions related to the caller’s query are displayed. These insights support the agent in promptly addressing user needs.
    

**Feedback on Recommendations**

You can provide feedback on the recommendations, which are recorded for analytics and reporting. If a recommendation meets your needs, you can give it a positive (thumbs up) rating, indicating that the provided information is accurate and complete.

If the recommendation is lacking, a negative rating prompts you to leave specific feedback comments detailing the issues or gaps you observed. This feedback helps improve the recommendation process through continuous training and refinement.

## Source: https://docs.avaamo.com/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view/summary

The `Call Summarization` feature in Avaamo Agent Assist streamlines post-call workflows by generating concise summaries of long conversations. This capability is designed to help agents quickly capture essential details and decisions, ensuring that important information is easily accessible for future reference.

This saves agents time, enhances information accuracy, and ensures that key insights are preserved for future use, improving both customer service and record-keeping efficiency.

Once a call ends, `Agent Assist` automatically generates a summary of the entire conversation. The summary includes the following elements:

*   **Caller Name**: Identifies the name of the caller from the conversation.
    
*   **Call Reasons**: Lists one or more reasons for the caller’s contact with the helpdesk.
    
*   **Call Tags**: Assign labels to categorize each conversation for better insights and quick retrieval of call records.
    
*   **Agent Actions**: Documents the various actions the agent performed during the call, such as verifying the user, looking up a patient record, or sending a nurse.
    
*   **Agent Promises**: Records any commitments you made that require follow-up, outlining action items to be fulfilled for the caller after the call concludes.
    
*   **Call Sentiment**: Displays the overall sentiment detected during the call. This sentiment reflects how the conversation progressed and concluded; a call that ends positively shows a positive sentiment, while a call that does not end well may be marked as negative.
    
*   **Summary**: Provides a brief overview generated by Agent Assist, capturing the key points of the conversation while excluding any sensitive information.
    

**How to use Call Summarization:**

After a call ends, Agent Assist automatically generates a summary within a few seconds and redirects the agent to the **Summary** tab. Here, agents can review, edit, and take action on the call summary.

1.  **View Call Summary**: The Summary tab displays an overview of the agent's most recent call.
    
2.  **Provide Feedback on Summary**: Agents have the option to give [feedback](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view#feedback-on-the-summary) on the summary, contributing to improved accuracy and relevance in future summaries.
    
3.  **Edit Summary**: Agents can customize the summary by clicking the [Edit Summary](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view#editing-the-summary) button, allowing them to make necessary adjustments to ensure accuracy and relevance.
    
4.  **Add Summary to CRM**: By selecting the [Add to CRM](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view#pushing-the-summary-into-the-ticket) button, agents can directly transfer the summary to a ticket within their CRM system, streamlining post-call documentation.
    
5.  **Copy Summary**: The summary can be [copied and pasted](/user-guide/avaamo-agent-assist/agent-assist-widget/ai-assist-view#copy-to-clipboard) as text for use in other applications or notes, enabling easy sharing and reference.
    

#### 

**Feedback on the Summary**

You can provide feedback on the automatically generated summary, which is recorded for analytics and reporting. If the summary meets your expectations, you can give it a positive (thumbs up) rating, indicating that the details were captured accurately with no significant gaps.

If you provide negative feedback, you are prompted to include specific comments explaining any issues or gaps you noticed. This feedback helps improve the tool through continuous training, identifying areas that need enhancement.

#### 

**Editing the Summary**

You can edit the summary generated by the Agent Assist tool. Each element or field of the summary can be edited as text. To make changes, click on the `Edit Summary` button, which presents an editable version of the entire summary. You can then modify any of the summary elements if you feel that the tool either missed or incorrectly captured details.

#### 

**Pushing the Summary into the Ticket**

The summary generated by Agent Assist can be directly pushed into the ticket associated with the call, thanks to API integration with systems like Salesforce or ServiceNow. This allows for a seamless, one-click process to update the ticket with the call summary.

To add the summary to a Salesforce or ServiceNow ticket:

*   Simply click on the `Add to CRM` button located at the bottom of the Summary view.
    
*   After confirming this action, the summary adds to the corresponding Interaction or Case fields in ServiceNow
    

#### 

**Copy to Clipboard**

If you prefer not to use the `Add to CRM` option, you can manually copy the summary using the `Copy to Clipboard` feature. This option is available in the top-right corner of the Summary view. When clicked, it copies the entire summary as formatted text, which can then be pasted into a text editor or manually into the ticket.

### 

Handling New Call Transcripts

The **Agent Assist** widget does not automatically refresh to show the latest call transcript from the previous call during wrap-up activities. Instead, you receive a notification that also acts as a link or button, allowing you to switch to the new conversation at your convenience manually. This feature is designed to prevent interruptions during the wrap-up of your current call.

By clicking on the `You have a new call`, the content view refreshes to display the transcript of the new call. If the new call ends before you switch to it, the entire conversation transcript and summary is still be available for review. You can revisit this missed conversation later.

### 

**What Happens When a Call Ends?**

The screen automatically switches to the Summary View when a call concludes. The summary is typically generated within 3-5 seconds, depending on the length of the conversation.

### 

**How to Stop the Transcription Midway**

You can stop the transcription of an ongoing call at any time by clicking the `End Transcription` button. After clicking, a prompt asks you to confirm your action. Once confirmed, the transcription stops immediately, and the call summary is generated based on the conversation up to that point.

### 

Overview

Avaamo maintains a comprehensive Information Security Management System (ISMS) that defines the policies, procedures, standards, and operational controls required to protect customer data and platform services. Roles and responsibilities are clearly defined across Product Security, Security Operations, Computer Security Incident Response, and Risk and Compliance functions to ensure effective governance and oversight.

### 

Security program

Avaamo's security team manages the following core functions of the ISMS.

#### 

Product security

The Product Security team is responsible for:

*   Establishing secure development practices and standards
    
*   Conducting module-level security risk assessments
    
*   Performing security design reviews and code reviews to identify and remediate security vulnerabilities
    
*   Providing secure coding training to developers
    

#### 

Security operations

The Security Operations team is responsible for:

*   Building and maintaining security-critical infrastructure
    
*   Managing security monitoring, alerting, and authentication services
    
*   Maintaining security-relevant audit logs
    
*   Ensuring secure configuration and maintenance of production environments
    

#### 

Computer security incident response

The Computer Security Incident Response Team (CSIRT) is responsible for:

*   Responding to security alerts and events
    
*   Managing security incidents
    
*   Acquiring and analyzing threat intelligence
    

#### 

Risk and compliance

The Risk and Compliance team is responsible for:

*   Coordinating penetration testing activities
    
*   Managing vulnerability scanning and remediation
    
*   Conducting risk assessments and tracking risk treatment plans
    
*   Managing security awareness programs
    
*   Coordinating audits and maintaining security certifications
    
*   Responding to customer security inquiries
    

### 

Policies and standards

Avaamo maintains a comprehensive set of policies, standards, procedures, and guidelines that govern the operation of its ISMS. These documents help ensure secure operations and establish expectations for ethical and compliant behavior across the organization.

The security documentation includes, but is not limited to:

*   Fair, ethical, and legal standards of business conduct
    
*   Acceptable use of information systems
    
*   Security record retention requirements
    
*   Business continuity and disaster recovery planning
    
*   Security incident classification and management
    
*   Change management controls
    
*   Information asset classification, labeling, and handling procedures
    
*   Workforce identification, authentication, and authorization requirements
    
*   Secure system development, acquisition, configuration, and maintenance practices
    
*   Workforce training and compliance requirements
    
*   Encryption standards and usage guidelines
    
*   Security assessments, including risk assessments, audits, and penetration testing
    
*   Third-party service provider management
    

These policies and standards are reviewed periodically, updated as required, and made available to all applicable personnel.

### 

Overview

Avaamo Agent Assist follows a privacy-by-design architecture to ensure secure handling of voice interactions and sensitive customer data. The platform processes only the data required to provide Agent Assist functionality while maintaining strict controls around audio handling, storage, and retention.

### 

Call processing scope

To ensure strict data segregation and privacy controls:

*   Avaamo receives call streams through SIPREC integrations.
    
*   Agent Assist validates the agent associated with the call before any data is processed.
    
*   Only calls associated with agents configured for Agent Assist are processed.
    
*   Calls associated with agents that are not configured for Agent Assist are discarded and never processed.
    

Processing is limited to real-time speech-to-text transcription and conversation analysis required to generate Agent Assist features such as call summaries and recommendations.

### 

Audio processing

Avaamo does not permanently store raw audio files.

*   Audio is received as a real-time stream.
    
*   Audio is processed temporarily in memory for real-time transcription.
    
*   Audio is handled in small segments during processing and is removed immediately after transcription is completed.
    
*   Raw call audio is never written to disk or retained after processing.
    

### 

Sensitive data handling

Agent Assist includes automated mechanisms to identify and remove sensitive information from conversation transcripts.

This includes:

*   Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
    
*   Electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI)
    
*   Payment card information (PCI)
    

Standard configurations perform real-time detection and redaction of sensitive data from transcripts. Any remaining sensitive information held temporarily in memory is permanently removed when the call ends.

### 

Data retention

Only sanitized transcripts and associated call metadata are retained.

Retained data is used to support:

*   Conversation analysis
    
*   Call summarization
    
*   Next-best-action recommendations
    
*   Auditing and compliance requirements
    
*   Historical reporting
    

All data transmitted to external systems, including CRM platforms, is secured using industry-standard encrypted communication (TLS 1.2 or higher).

Processed transcript data and metadata are retained for up to two years by default.

### 

Data security and encryption

Retained data is protected using industry-standard encryption controls.

*   Data in transit is protected using TLS 1.2 or higher encryption.
    
*   Data at rest is protected using AES-256 encryption.
    
*   Access to retained data is governed by Avaamo's security and compliance controls.
    

### 

Secure data purging

Avaamo follows National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) guidelines for data masking and secure data destruction.

At the end of the retention period:

*   Stored data is permanently removed.
    
*   Secure deletion methods are used to ensure the data cannot be recovered.
    
*   Deleted data is rendered permanently unrecoverable.
    

These controls ensure that retained data cannot be reconstructed, restored, or accessed once the retention period has expired.
